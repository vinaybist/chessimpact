Introduction

Chess Impact was founded with the goal of fostering a vibrant chess community. Our club provides a welcoming environment for players of all ages and skill levels to learn, play, and grow in their chess journey.

Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced player seeking challenging opponents, our club offers resources, events, and a supportive community to help you enjoy and improve at chess.

Club Mission & Information

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote chess as an educational tool that enhances critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and sportsmanship. We aim to make chess accessible to everyone in our community through regular meetings, tournaments, and educational programs.

Club Information

Meeting Schedule: We meet every Wednesday from 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM.

Location: Bluffview Community Center, Mongolain Dr, Leander, TX.

Activities: Regular club play, tournaments, free chess lessons, and special events.
